## ğŸ¯ Problem Statement

In many regions, including Tanzania:

- Wills are stored physically in law firms, warehouses, tapes, CDs, flash drives, or handwritten documents
- Physical storage introduces risks such as fire, loss, damage, and unauthorized access
- Retrieval is slow and often stressful for beneficiaries
- Verification processes are manual and fragmented

Despite these challenges, there is no centralized and secure digital system handling wills responsibly at scale.

---

## ğŸ’¡ Proposed Solution

DELVATZ provides a **secure, encrypted digital will vault** where:

- Individuals store wills and legacy materials digitally
- Law firms manage and validate documents through the system
- Beneficiaries are verified using legally recognized documents
- Access is controlled, logged, and auditable

Supported formats include:
- Written documents (PDF, scans, text)
- Audio wills
- Video wills
- Digitized archives from physical media
